How to Play Scoundrel

1

Start the game

Press New Game. Four cards are dealt face-up in the room. Your hero starts with 20 HP.

2

Resolve 3 of the 4 cards

Click any card to interact with it. You must resolve exactly 3 cards — the one you leave behind carries forward into the next room.

  • ♥ Potion — heals you by its value (max 20 HP). Only one potion heals per room; extras are discarded.
  • ♦ Weapon — equip it. Your old weapon is discarded.
  • ♣ / ♠ Monster — you take damage equal to its value unless your weapon's value is higher (see below).
3

Using your weapon against monsters

If you have a weapon equipped, it absorbs damage: damage = monster value − weapon value (minimum 0).

⚠️ The weapon can only fight a monster whose value is equal to or lower than the last monster it defeated. If the new monster is stronger, you fight bare-handed and take full damage.

Example: Weapon ♦7 defeats ♣9 → 2 damage taken. Next you can fight ♣9 or lower using the weapon. A ♠J would deal 11 damage bare-handed.

4

Or avoid the room

Press Avoid Room to send all 4 cards to the bottom of the deck and draw a fresh room. You cannot avoid two rooms in a row.

5

Win or lose

  • 🏆 Win — clear the entire deck. Score = your remaining HP.
  • 💀 Lose — HP drops to 0. Score = negative monster total still in deck.

The cards

44 cards total — red face cards and red Aces are removed from a standard deck:

  • ♣ ♠ 2–A → 26 monsters
  • ♦ 2–10 → 9 weapons
  • ♥ 2–10 → 9 potions
