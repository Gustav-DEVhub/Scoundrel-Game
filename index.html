How to Play Scoundrel

The Deck

44 cards remain after removing Jokers, red face cards, and red Aces:

  • Monsters ♣ ♠ 2–A (26 cards) — deal damage
  • Weapons ♦ 2–10 (9 cards) — reduce damage
  • Potions ♥ 2–10 (9 cards) — restore health

Your health starts at 20 and cannot exceed 20.

Each Turn — The Room

Four cards are revealed. You must either Avoid or Face the room.

Avoid

All 4 cards go to the bottom of the deck. You cannot avoid two rooms in a row.

Face the Room

Choose and resolve exactly 3 of the 4 cards in any order. The un-chosen card carries forward into the next room as card #1.

Resolving Cards

Potion ♥

Heal by the card's value (capped at 20). Only one potion per room heals you — extras are discarded.

Weapon ♦

Equip it: your old weapon (and its defeated-monster stack) is discarded. The weapon's "last defeated" resets to none.

Monster ♣ / ♠

  • Bare-handed: take damage equal to the monster's value.
  • With weapon: damage = max(0, monster − weapon).
    The weapon can only be used if monster value ≤ last defeated monster's value (or if no monster has been defeated yet).
    Example: weapon ♦7 defeats ♣9 (damage 2), then can fight ♣9 or lower. Fighting ♠J bare-handed after that still costs 11 hp.

Winning & Losing

  • Win — clear the dungeon deck. Score = remaining health.
  • Lose — health reaches 0. Score = negative sum of remaining monster values.
